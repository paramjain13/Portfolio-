Elara — NLP System for Edge

Most NLP models are too heavy to run on everyday devices, and that bugged me. Elara is a question-answering system I built by fine-tuning DistilBERT on SQuAD 2.0 — it hits 91% F1 while being lightweight enough for edge hardware. The real challenge was shrinking the model from 2GB down to 500MB through knowledge distillation without tanking the accuracy. I also set up a benchmarking pipeline to test 8 different inference configurations so I could find the sweet spot between speed and quality.

HuggingFace DistilBERT Knowledge Distillation Edge AI
2026

Hidato Puzzle Solver — AI Search

A fun one from my AI coursework. Hidato is a logic puzzle where you fill a grid with consecutive numbers, each adjacent to the next. I framed it as a constraint satisfaction problem and built multiple solvers to compare how different search strategies perform — basic DFS with backtracking, a heuristic-guided version with pruning, and A* search. The interesting part was seeing how drastically heuristics cut down the search space. Includes a step-by-step visualization so you can watch each solver think its way through the grid.

Python A* Search CSP DFS Heuristics
Nov 2024 – Dec 2024

Diabetic Retinopathy Detection

Early detection of diabetic retinopathy can prevent blindness — that's what motivated this one. I trained an EfficientNet-B4 on a dataset of 92K retinal scans and got it to 89.7% accuracy after a lot of iterative tuning. The trickiest part was making the model small enough for real-world deployment; quantization-aware training helped me cut the size by 60% without losing much accuracy. Distributed training across 4 GPUs brought the training time down from 18 hours to about 7.

PyTorch EfficientNet CUDA Scikit-learn
